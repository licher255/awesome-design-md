### Key Features

- **Void Black Background** (`#0a0a0b`) - Deep space darkness
- **Marathon Lime** (`#c2fe0b`) - Toxic yellow-green primary accent
- **Cyan Accent** (`#01ffff`) - Secondary sci-fi glow color
- **Alert Red** (`#ff0d1a`) - Danger/warning signals
- **Industrial Typography** - Condensed military stencil fonts
- **Dot Matrix & Monospace** - Technical data readout aesthetics
- **Chamfered Shapes** - Cut-corner polygons for industrial feel
- **Tactical Interface** - Styled like a combat helmet HUD

## Design Principles

1. **Dark & Ominous** - Void blacks with aggressive neon accents
2. **Industrial Military** - Chamfered corners, stencil typography
3. **Graphic Realism** - Simplified design with realistic proportions
4. **Tactical Information** - Clear hierarchy like combat HUDs
5. **Mystery & Danger** - Colors convey threat and opportunity
